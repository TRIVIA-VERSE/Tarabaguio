Welcome to Baguio

Baguio City is a mountain resort in the Philippines known for its cool weather, pine trees, and scenic views. A great escape from the tropical heat.

Why Visit Baguio?

  • Experience the famous Burnham Park and Session Road.
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty of Mines View Park and Botanical Garden.
  • Learn about the rich Cordilleran culture and traditions.
  • Try unique local dishes like strawberry taho and ukoy.
  • Pick fresh strawberries in La Trinidad’s Strawberry Farm.

Best Time to Visit

The cool climate makes Baguio a great destination year-round, but it's especially popular during the dry season (November to May) and Panagbenga Flower Festival (February).
