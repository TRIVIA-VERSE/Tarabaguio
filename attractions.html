Top Attractions in Baguio

Burnham Park

A popular spot for boating, biking, and relaxing strolls amidst gardens and pine trees.

Mines View Park

Offers stunning views of the mining town of Itogon and nearby mountains.

Wright Park

Famous for its Pool of Pines and horseback riding area for tourists of all ages.

Botanical Garden

Explore native plants and Igorot huts that celebrate Cordillera culture and ecology.

Strawberry Farm (La Trinidad)

Pick fresh strawberries straight from the farm just a few minutes outside the city.

The Mansion

The official summer residence of the Philippine President, known for its ornate gates and gardens.

Camp John Hay

Once a US military base, now a leisure resort with golf courses, hotels, and hiking trails.

Session Road

The heart of Baguio's city life, filled with cafes, shops, and local charm.

Baguio Cathedral

An iconic pink church perched on a hill, offering city views and spiritual serenity.

Igorot Stone Kingdom

A relatively new attraction inspired by legends and traditional Igorot architecture.
